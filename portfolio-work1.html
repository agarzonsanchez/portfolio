My Work

Java Video Game

Technologies used: Java, GUI

Academic Video game designed to explain how C-Elegans nematode is important for rivers. This Java program has a GUI easy to interact to and play. It is part of a final thesis for a group of students in the Genetics progam from the Colegio Mayor de Cundinamarca University - Bogota - Colombia.

It has 9 different video games where users can interact and understand the importance of C-elegans nematode. (Word search, Maze, Drag and drop, Crossword, Hangman )
